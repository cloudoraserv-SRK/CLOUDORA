Frequently Asked Questions

Which blockchains do you work with?

We primarily specialize in **EVM-compatible** blockchains like Ethereum, Polygon, and BNB Chain, but we can also work with other ecosystems like Solana or Avalanche on a custom basis.

Do you offer security audits?

Yes. Every smart contract we develop undergoes an in-depth internal security review. For high-value projects, we facilitate and work closely with leading third-party audit firms like ConsenSys or CertiK.

Can you help with a token launch?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive support for token launches, including **tokenomics design**, smart contract creation (e.g., ERC-20), and integration with launchpads or decentralized exchanges.
